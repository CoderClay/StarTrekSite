One of my most early, most cherished, memories is walking into the living room and seeing my father laid back on the couch watching Star Trek. I couldn't have been more than six or seven, but I can still picture the vivid colors and hear the dramatic music in my minds eye. The firs character that comes to memory is that of a lanky dark-haired man with a blue shirt and weird ears. I couldn't have imagined then how impactful Star Trek would be on my life or how, as the years progressed, it would always be there waiting for me as a comforting reminder of home.
